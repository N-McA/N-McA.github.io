Location

On Tufnell Park Road;  N70PS

A 10 minute walk to Tufnell Park Station (Northern line) and a 15 minute walk to Holloway Station (Piccadilly line).

15 minutes by bike to King’s Cross, and 20 minutes to UCL; 15 minutes from the Castle climbing center.

House culture

Every Monday we host house dinner and invite lots of guests - often it’s around 14 or so people.

We generally are quite communal with food, in general with a shared weekly grocery shop. Most of our food is vegetarian or vegan.

Current housemates have academic backgrounds in machine learning, neuroscience, physics and philosophy. New housemates welcome whether their interests overlap with ours or totally different 😊
